What is Godhand?

Godhand is a fantasy tabletop roleplaying game system in development that combines the best aspects of popular TTRPG systems.

Godhand uses a structured set of rules to establish how things work in game, and players roll dice (usually a 20 sided dice) to determine the outcomes of actions they take in game. Each player creates their own character, a hero (or perhaps a villain?) and those characters set off, in most cases teaming up and working together on adventures, changing their world in ways profound.

It’s impossible for a game of Godhand to happen though, without someone to run it! But not just run it, to look after it, and nurture it, and love it very much. One special player fills that role, the Game Master or “GM” is responsible for overseeing the game and all that happens in it. They tell the brunt of story, serve as a sort of referee for the table and the rules. Without the GM the game is impossible, as a player might describe themselves creating a powerful explosion with magic, but without the GM there will be no reason for the explosion to be cast. There will be no goons to catch in the explosion, no idyllic pasture to set aflame with the fire created by the magic, no story to be told after the party burns down a sacred forest and needs to go on the run from a variety of very angry fae creatures!

The GM will also offer up threads to the players. Pieces of story to follow, or objectives to complete. They run the game, however the game itself has no hard endpoint. A group might play one game, and then another, and another in a long story that stretches out in the form of a campaign. Games happen when players and a GM come to sit down and play together, to roll dice, get into character, and have a good time. A campaign is each of those games together, collected as one (semi) unified story. Campaigns can end, likely after the completion of some great objective or groundbreaking event. Battling back an evil that threatens the world, gaining great fame and power, or the death of all the heroes at once.

This site provides a comprehensive repository of the Godhand system’s rules for character creation, combat, equipment, and so much more.
