Guide to Spell Lists

The spells are sorted by spellcasting class and then by the spells' level and name. Each spell will provide entry listed will have a brief description, spell range, the school of magic, etc. Clicking on the spell name will show a detailed description of the spell in question, such as rules for spell and the duration of the spell.
