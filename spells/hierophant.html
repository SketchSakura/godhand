Bane

Range: 30 feet Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute 1st-level Enchantment
Up to three creatures of your choice that you can see within range must make Charisma saving throws. Invoke Mortality  |  60 ft  |  1 action  V   |   Necromancy

Target makes Wisdom saving throw or be frightened.

Invoke Mortality

Casting Time: 1 action Casting Components: V
Range: 60 feet Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute 1st-level Necromancy
You awaken the sense of mortality in one creature you can see within range, making it acutely aware of just how simple it would be for it to die. A construct or undead with less than 8 intelligence is immune to this effect. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or else become frightened of you until the spell ends. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 1st. The creatures must be within 30 feet of each other when you target them.

At Higher Levels: This spell deals an additional d6 of damage for every level above 1st it‚Äôs cast.

Sanctuary  |  30 ft  |  1 bonus action  S | M (a little pouch of ashes)   |   Abjuration

Create war

Sanctuary

Casting Time: 1 bonus action Casting Components: S M (a little pouch of ashes)
Range: 30 feet Duration: Instantaneous 1st-level Abjuration
You quickly create a series of intricate wards over a target, that manifest in some way on their body, or in the air around them. While warded in this way, any creature who would attack the target must make a wisdom saving throw against your spell save DC. If they fail, they must attack another creature within their range, if there they have no other creature within range, the attack fails. This does not protect the target from area of effect spells (such as explosion).

If the warded creature makes an attack, casts a spell that affects an enemy, or deals damage to another creature, this spell ends.

At Higher Levels: When cast at 3rd level or higher, when the warded creature attacks, casts a spell that effects an enemy, or deals damage to another creature, the caster of this spell may make a check with their casting ability against their own spell save DC. If the check succeeds, the spell remains. (The casting ability and DC used are the one of the class the character used to cast this spell. A multiclasses Hierophant and Wizard makes this check with their wisdom modifier against their Hierophant save DC, for example.)

Shelter from Darkness

Casting Time: 1 action Casting Components: S
Range: Touch Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes 1st-level Abjuration
For the duration, all fiends, undead, aberrations, celestials, and fae have disadvantage on attacks to hit a creature under the effects of this spell, and cannot charm, frighten, or possess them.

Ward  |  30 ft  |  1 reaction  V | S   |   Abjuration

Reduce the amount of damage on a creature being hit by an attack.

Ward

Casting Time: 1 reaction Casting Components: V S
Range: 30 feet Duration: Instantaneous 1st-level Abjuration
You reach out the moment a creature is hit by an attack, placing protective wards across their body. The damage of the triggering attack is reduced by 5.
