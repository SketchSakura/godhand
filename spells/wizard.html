Fog Cloud  |  120 ft  |  1 action  V | S | M (A single ice cube)   |   Transmutation

Create a 20-ft sphere of obscuring fog at a point in range

Fog Cloud

Casting Time: 1 action Casting Components: V S M (A single ice cube)
Range: 120 feet Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour 1st-level Transmutation
When you cast this spell, you instantly force the water in an area to condense rapidly. You create a 20-foot-radius sphere of fog centered on a point within range. The sphere spreads around corners, and its area is heavily obscured. It lasts for the duration or until a wind of moderate or greater speed (at least 10 miles per hour) disperses it.

At higher levels: When you cast this spell at 3rd level or higher, you treat the area of the fog as being lightly obscured so long as you concentrate on the spell. If you lose concentration, you treat the fog as being heavily obscured, but the fog remains for the duration without you concentrating on the spell.

Grease  |  60 ft  |  1 action  V | S | M (a bit of pork rind or butter)   |   Conjuration

Create slick grease in a 10-foot square at a point within range

Grease

Casting Time: 1 action Casting Components: V S M (a bit of pork rind or butter)
Range: 60 feet Duration: 1 minute 1st-level Conjuration
Slick grease covers the ground in a 10-foot square centered on a point within range and turns it into difficult terrain for the duration. When the grease appears, each creature standing in its area must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or fall prone. A creature that enters the area or ends its turn there must also succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or fall prone.

Additionally, if fire damage is dealt to the grease at any point in time, it erupts violently into flame, and any creatures standing in the grease take the damage dealt to the grease and begin burning (as the burning condition). Dealing this damage burns all the grease away, ending the spell.

Invoke Mortality  |  60 ft  |  1 action  V   |   Necromancy

Target makes Wisdom saving throw or be frightened.

Invoke Mortality

Casting Time: 1 action Casting Components: V
Range: 60 feet Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute 1st-level Necromancy
You awaken the sense of mortality in one creature you can see within range, making it acutely aware of just how simple it would be for it to die. A construct or undead with less than 8 intelligence is immune to this effect. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or else become frightened of you until the spell ends. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 1st. The creatures must be within 30 feet of each other when you target them.

Jet of Flame

Casting Time: 1 action Casting Components: V S

Throwing out a hand, a torrent of raging flame erupts from it! Each creature in a 15-foot cone must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 2d6 fire damage on a failed save and gains the burning condition. Creatures who succeed on the save take half damage and do not begin burning.

The fire ignites any flammable objects in the area that aren‚Äôt being worn or carried.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the initial damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.

Lesser Charm  |  30 ft  |  1 action  V | S   |   Enchantment

Short description of the spell

Lesser Charm

Casting Time: 1 action Casting Components: V S
Range: 30 feet Duration: 1 hour 1st-level Enchantment
You subtly manipulate the mind of another to cause them to view you in a more inherently favorable light. One target within range must make a wisdom saving throw, or be charmed by you for the duration. If you or your allies are fighting the creature, it makes the saving throw with advantage. A creature charmed by you in this way will carry out any simple course of action you suggest to it, so long as that course of action does not require any great deal of effort, or meaningful risk on the part of the creature. When the spell ends, the creature knows it was charmed by you.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 1st. The creatures must be within 30 feet of each other when you target them.

Pestilent Cloud  |  30 ft  |  1 action  V | S | M (a piece of unclean cloth)   |   Conjuration

Fill a 10-ft radius air of space with unclean miasma

Pestilent Cloud

Casting Time: 1 action Casting Components: V S M (a piece of unclean cloth)
Range: 30 feet Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute 1st-level Conjuration
You fill the air of a space within range with a 10 foot radius sphere of unclean miasma. Any creature that breathes within the area must make a constitution saving throw, or take 1d8 viral damage, and become poisoned for the duration. A creature that starts its turn in the sphere must make the saving throw again or take another 1d8 viral damage.

Shield

Range: Self Duration: Special (Instantaneous or 1 Round, chosen at casting) 1st-level Abjuration

When you cast this spell, you can choose to gain either +2 to AC until the start of your next turn, or +5 AC against a single attack.
